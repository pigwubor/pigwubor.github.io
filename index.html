Profile Summary


Igwubor Philip Chukwunwike is a Nigerian by birth and origin; he graduated with a degree honour in Computer Science & Statistics from University of Nigeria, Nsukka. He also bagged a diploma in Mechanical Engineering, from the prestigious polytechnic, Yaba College of Technology.


He has more than 8 years working experience, with 4 years in E-Banking sector and 4 years with ICT-Payment Solution Provider. His career roles and achievements has cut across major facet of ICT project implementation with finance and non-finance sectors, automate & implement process flow, project management, system analysis and support, E-banking and e-commerce, web development, Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) and servicing high power tech devices and computing systems for banks, telecoms and other government parastatals.
